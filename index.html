Every 10¢ or 20¢ from your Grab ride or order goes towards projects that help Mother Nature.

Here's what happens when you opt in to our Green Programme:

Turn your small change into big changes for the planet

Old cooling systems usually contain harmful refrigerant gases. Your support helps A-Gas Singapore’s Ozone Depleting Substance Destruction Project collect and destroy them.
By backing this project, you're directly helping fight global warming. Now, that’s really cool.
Did you know?

Your old ACs contain gases that are climate ‘bombs’

The gases are thousands of times more damaging than Co₂. A-Gas uses cutting-edge technology to eliminate them safely.
Fun fact: Seagrass meadows can store more carbon than tropical rainforests!
Seagrass (yup...) is an underwater plant that supports marine life, protects coastlines, and stores carbon. You'll be helping us fund Singapore’s first seagrass nursery.
Together, Grab, WWF, and NUS-TMSI are working to restore these precious meadows, by creating a model for large-scale restoration across Southeast Asia.
Did you know?

Grass also grows at sea. Really.

That’s not all.

The Green Programme also supports CarbonCure in Singapore, turning CO₂ into stronger concrete, cutting emissions, and reducing cement use.

 

And over 3,000km away in Indonesia, your contribution helps conserve the Katingan Mentaya Project peat swamp forest too.
